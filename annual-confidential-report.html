Arkivo is dedicated to simplifying the ACR process, making it electronic and online. With a comprehensive set of features tailored to meet the specific needs of government agencies, our ACR management system streamlines the entire process from submission to evaluation.

Key Features

Features of an Annual Confidential Report (ACR) Management System

  • User Management

    Admins can create and manage user profiles for employees, evaluators, and administrators. Assign roles and permissions to control access to various system functions.

  • Electronic ACR Submission

    Employees can submit ACRs electronically through the system. Attach supporting documents and evidence to their reports. Set submission deadlines and receive notifications.

  • Customizable Templates

    Create and customize ACR templates based on the specific evaluation criteria of different departments or organizations. Modify templates easily to accommodate changing requirements

  • Multi-Level Approval Workflow

    Define a multi-level approval workflow that mirrors the hierarchy of the organization. Automatic routing of ACRs to the appropriate evaluators and approvers. Configurable rules for evaluations, including weightage, scoring, and comments.

  • Automated Reminders

    Send automated reminders to employees and evaluators to complete and submit ACRs on time. Escalation of reminders for overdue submissions.

  • Data Security

    Implement robust data encryption and access controls to protect sensitive ACR data. Comply with data privacy regulations and maintain confidentiality.
