In the realm of sustainable forest resource management, innovation is the key to a prosperous and eco-conscious future. At Arkivo, we are proud to introduce our revolutionary solution - the Integrated Forest Produce Management System. This powerful system has been meticulously designed to digitize and streamline the entire forest produce supply chain, ushering in a new era of efficiency, transparency, and sustainability. It offers advanced features to ensure seamless operations and streamlined decision-making.

What we offer

  • Marking of Trees

    Precise Tree Identification for Sustainable Forestry

    Our system initiates with the accurate marking of trees in the forest. Each tree is tagged with critical data, including species, grade, and location. This process supports sustainable forestry practices and ensures the precise identification of trees ready for harvest.

  • Logging Management

    Efficient Timber Extraction and Accountability

    Logging and timber extraction are streamlined with our mobile-based logging management system. Mobile devices empower your workforce to capture real-time data during extraction, including timber species and grades. This feature guarantees precise inventory management and fosters accountability.

  • Transportation to Depots

    Optimized Transportation with Transit Pass Generation

    Our system optimizes transportation routes using GPS technology, reducing costs and minimizing environmental impact. It includes the critical feature of Transit Pass generation in the field, ensuring legal compliance and efficient timber movement.

  • Species and Grade-wise Inventory Management at Depots

    Efficient Inventory Control for Maximum Utilization

    At your depots, our system facilitates meticulous management of diverse timber species and grades. Quality control measures are in place to preserve wood quality, while real-time inventory tracking maintains accurate stock levels. Maximize storage space utilization while minimizing waste.

  • Sales Management

    Customized Sales for Diverse Timber

    Our Sales Management module simplifies the sales process by offering features like stack and lot creation. Customize lots based on timber species and grades to meet specific customer demands. The system calculates floor prices for lots based on historical data, business rules and market dynamics, ensuring fair pricing and revenue maximization.

  • E-Auction of Forest Produce

    Advanced Auction Features for Enhanced Transparency

    Our E-Auction module introduces advanced features, including auto-bidding, to enhance transparency in the auction process. Bidders can participate from anywhere, ensuring a competitive marketplace.

  • Post Auction Rule-based Lot Approval

    Efficient Post-Auction Decision Making

    After the auction, our system enables rule-based lot approval, ensuring expedited decisions. Winning bidders receive prompt information, enhancing their confidence in the process.

  • Online Security and Balance Payment Collection

    Secure Transactions and Payment Collection

    Our system prioritizes online security and allows for the collection of balance payments from winning bidders after lot approval. Your transactions are safe and efficient.

  • E-Invoicing and Exit Pass

    Automated Invoicing and Exit Pass Generation

    E-Invoicing streamlines the invoicing process, and the system generates exit passes for efficient timber movement tracking and compliance.

  • Accounting and Finance

    Comprehensive Financial Management

    Our Accounting and Finance module includes robust bookkeeping features, ensuring complete control over your financial transactions.

  • HRMS and Payroll

    Efficient Personnel Management and Payroll Automation

    Manage your workforce with ease through our HRMS and Payroll module. Streamline payroll processes and ensure accurate compensation for your employees.

  • Electronic Note Approval

    Expedited Decision Making

    Streamline decision-making with our Electronic Note Approval system. Expedite the approval process for critical decisions.

Arkivo's Integrated Forest Produce Management System empowers you to unlock the full potential of your timber resources.
